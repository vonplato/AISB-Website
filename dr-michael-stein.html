Michael Z. Stein, MD

Dr. Stein graduated from Macalester College in St. Paul, Minnesota. He attended medical school at the University of Minnesota, completed his internship at MacNeal Hospital in Illinois, and his residency at Indiana University. Dr. Stein also did a Breast Imaging Fellowship at Indiana University. Dr. Stein's interests are in breast imaging and general radiology. Since 2006, he has served as the Director of Women's Imaging at the PLCMMCT Women's Imaging Center.
