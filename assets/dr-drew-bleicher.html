Drew Bleicher, MD

Dr. Bleicher is a Board-Certified Diagnostic Radiologist and Interventional Radiologist at Providence Little Company of Mary. He is a graduate of the University of Miami where he obtained a degree in Biology. He then completed medical school at the University of Miami Miller School of Medicine. This was followed by Radiology Residency as well as Neuroradiology and Interventional Radiology Fellowships at University of Miami and Jackson Memorial Hospital.
His interests include interventional oncology, MRI, women’s health, prostate artery embolization, interventional pain and venous disease. Dr. Bleicher has published in basic science and medical journals as well as presented at society meetings.

He is happy to be living in Marina Del Rey. In his leisure, he enjoys exercising, hiking, being on the water, and traveling.
